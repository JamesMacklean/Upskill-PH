ICT WORKFORCE DEVELOPMENT PROGRAM GUIDELINES

You are applying as one of the learners for the DICT ILCDB ICT Workforce Development led by the DICT with its solution providers MOOCSX, and Coursera. The DICT shall cover the training expenses for the duration of your access.



If accepted, you are expected to perform, and be guided with the following:

  1. Complete your selected learning track within the approved duration of your access, details of which will be provided upon invite.
  2. Devote the necessary amount of time and carry out the activities as stated in the plan of activities;
  3. Conduct oneself professionally and use the knowledge gained in this training in the interest of the public good;
  4. Acknowledge the support of the DICT in all reports and any publication arising from the training, a copy of which should be provided to DICT;
  5. Participate in the training monitoring and evaluation survey to be conducted by the organizers;
  6. You may be tapped as resource person for related activities of the DICT in the future in a one-time free of charge service.
  7. DICT reserves the right to revoke your license access to Coursera should you be inactive without justifiable reason or voluntarily withdraw from the program in order to give your slot to other waitlisted participants.
